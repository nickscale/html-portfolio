About.

I've always been an engineer. Making demo disks on my Amiga 500. Building video editing PCs. Designing my homepage with a custom back-end CMS.

For my Electronic Engineering degree, I did software simulations of laboratory hardware in C++ and Shockwave (RIP).

My career turned to film post production. I streamed live video before YouTube existed. Over 20 years, I edited thousands of shorts, commercials, documentaries and feature films.

In 2023, I wanted a new challenge so I came back to my engineering roots. Now I'm using my software development skills to make my ideas real.

When I'm not coding, I'm writing short stories, producing electronic music, and making films.
