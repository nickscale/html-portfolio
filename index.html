About.

I'm a Junior Full Stack Developer in central London.

I've always been an engineer, from making demo disks on my Amiga 500, to building video editing PCs. For my engineering degree I made software simulations of laboratory hardware.

My first career was in video post production. I was live streaming before YouTube existed. For 20 years, I edited 1000s of commercials, TV shows and films.

In 2023, I wanted a new challenge so I returned to my engineering roots, and started turning my ideas into reality.

When I'm not coding, I write short stories, produce synthy noises and make short films.
