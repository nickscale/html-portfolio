About.

I'm a Junior Full Stack Developer based in central London.

I've always been an engineer, from making demo disks on my Amiga 500 to building video editing PCs.

For my Electronic Engineering degree, I made software simulations of lab hardware in C++ and Shockwave (RIP).

My career turned to video post production. I streamed live webcasts before YouTube existed. Over 20 years, I edited 1000s of shorts, commercials, factual TV and cinema films.

In 2023, I wanted a new challenge so I returned to my engineering roots. I love turning ideas into reality.

When I'm not coding I'm writing short stories, producing electronic music, and making films.
