picture lock
noun
  1. Final stage of editing before the locked cut is handed over to other departments such as sound post, grade, motion graphics and VFX.
  2. The finished offline edit. The timeline is locked such that no further changes are possible. All edits and timing are now considered fixed and frame accurate. This will be used as a reliable reference for all subsequent post production processes such as sound post, grade, motion graphics and VFX.
Related terms:
handover, final cut
